Our Mission

At Dumna Wildlife Sanctuary, we are dedicated to the vital mission of protecting endangered species and their habitats. We understand that every animal plays a crucial role in maintaining the delicate balance of our ecosystems. Our passionate team works tirelessly to rehabilitate injured and orphaned wildlife, providing them with the care and support they need to thrive again in their natural environments.

Together, we can make a difference. Our sanctuary serves as a safe haven for animals and a hub for conservation efforts. We invite you to join us in our mission to protect the beauty and diversity of wildlife for future generations. Together, we can create a world where all species can flourish and coexist in harmony.

Our Vision

At Dumna Wildlife Sanctuary, our vision is to create a world where all wildlife is protected and respected, where ecosystems flourish, and where humans and animals coexist in harmony. We envision a future in which every endangered species thrives in its natural habitat, free from the threats of habitat destruction, poaching, and environmental degradation. Our sanctuary serves as a model for sustainable conservation practices, showcasing how human intervention can lead to positive outcomes for wildlife and the planet.

We aspire to be a leading force in wildlife conservation, not only by rehabilitating and protecting endangered species but also by inspiring a global movement towards ecological responsibility. At Dumna Wildlife Sanctuary, we believe that with dedication, education, and compassion, we can inspire change and build a sustainable world where wildlife can thrive, ensuring a rich and diverse planet for generations to come.
